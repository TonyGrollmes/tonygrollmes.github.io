Midnight Highrise

A Neon-Soaked Parkour Escape

Level Design • First-Person Parkour • Unreal Engine

Tony Grollmes • December 2025

Level Story & Setting

A dense, neon-soaked city during a heavy nighttime rainstorm. The player wakes in a cramped basement maintenance room beneath a corporate district and must ascend through hidden service corridors, rooftops, and high-rise buildings to reach the Skyline Tower rooftop before security systems lock down.

  • Emphasizes verticality and constant upward movement
  • Mixture of tight interior passages and exposed rooftop segments
  • Wall-running and wall-clinging as core survival tools

Objectives

Primary Objective

Escape the basement and reach Skyline Tower rooftop using parkour movement

Optional Challenges

  • Data Collection: Explore risky alternate routes
  • Time Trial: Complete within 3 minutes
  • Perfect Run: Zero deaths from falls

Stressors & Challenges

Long-Fall Risk

Constant threat from basement to exposed rooftops. Missed jumps = lethal drops.

Hazardous Floors

Electrified panels and unstable grates force vertical movement.

Time Pressure

3-minute soft timer pushes toward high-risk shortcuts and aggressive routing.

Level Progression & Pacing

  • Lower Level: Basic jumps, intro wall-runs, short clings (Low intensity)
  • Street Rooftops: Longer gaps, first fall exposure, branching (Moderate intensity)
  • Rooftop Cluster: Chained sequences, layered hazards, high consequences (High intensity)
  • Skyline Tower: Hardest sequences and final climb to rooftop (Climactic intensity)

Custom Level Mechanics

Wall-Run

Horizontal runs along marked surfaces to cross gaps, bypass hazards, and chain movement between platforms.

Wall-Cling

Short-duration clings after jumps or wall-runs, giving brief pause points for reorientation before losing grip.

Visual Direction

  • Neon. Vertical. Rainy.
  • High-contrast cityscape with bright signage cutting through the dark
  • Glowing billboards and wet reflective rooftops to reinforce height and risk
  • Stacked architecture that pushes extreme sense of depth and verticality
  • Cyberpunk-inspired atmosphere with fast, tense pacing

Design Inspiration

Prince of Persia: Sands of Time

Parkour mechanics, wall-clinging, and vertical progression define the core movement language.

Titanfall 2

Wall-running, neon urban aesthetic, and high-speed movement create intensity and flow.
