Level Design Showcase - Unreal Engine Environment

Role: Level Designer

Tools: Unreal Engine

Description: A playable Unreal Engine environment built from a graybox layout into a fully lit, textured space. The level started as a blockout focused on sightlines, traversal routes, and encounter pacing, then was iterated with custom Blueprints to handle player interaction, doors, pickups, and simple gameplay logic. Materials, lighting, and props were layered in to reinforce navigation cues and landmarks, using modular meshes, post-processing, and polish passes to support readability, exploration, and smooth moment-to-moment flow.

Midnight Highrise – First-Person Parkour Level

Tools: Unreal Engine

Role: Level Designer

Description: A standalone first-person parkour level set in a neon-soaked city during a nighttime rainstorm. The player ascends from a cramped basement to the Skyline Tower rooftop using wall-running and wall-clinging while managing long-fall risk, hazardous floors, and soft time pressure.
